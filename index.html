Sunil Nair

Senior Product Manager

Sunil Nair is a SAFe POPM certified Senior Product Manager in Sapient’s Toronto office. He joined Sapient in April 2022 on the Jenny Craig account in the Retail-Health & Wellness space and a total work experience of 14 years in PM capability in Retail, CPG, Pharma, Financial Services and Aerospace industries. In the JC account working with business and stakeholders, Sunil was key in launching the website and react native mobile app on the Salesforce Commerce cloud platform. Right from discovery up to MVP launch and further and building key artefacts including BCD, Scope Matrix, Roadmap, Sunil has played an instrumental role in the building the platform and key features live on the site today. Outside of Sapient, most of the Sunil’s experience has been in hybrid projects primarily in waterfall-based development and scaling/enhancing them through lean agile methodologies. He has worked in manufacturing projects involving software and hardware development building support infrastructure and achieving deliverables for varied clients globally. His key skills include product development, developing vision and roadmap, running digital experiments, conducting extensive market research & analysis, handling cross-functional teams, presenting business cases, building case studies, marketing, and is a staunch customer advocate for delivering value. Sunil is immensely creative and proficient at ideation, building user journeys, business context diagrams, journey mapping, feature presentations, new feature proposal, developing mockups, sketches and holds a diploma in Animation and web-design. He has extensive knowledge of processes in supply chain, logistics, procurement processes in a manufacturing environment, ecommerce, catalog design, data management, ERP systems, merchandising solutions etc. and has hands-on experience working with buyers, channel partners, international suppliers, brokers and third parties. He holds a bachelor’s degree in Electronics and Instrumentation Engineering from University of Mumbai and currently continuing his education by pursuing the MicroMasters Program in Digital Leadership at Boston University. He has acquired expertise in Project Management with almost 10 years of experience working varied projects and has certifications including Prince2 Foundation and PMP certification is in progress. He is currently pursuing graduation at SPEED Academy, a 5-month cross-discipline practical program within Publicis Sapient NA to develop skills outside of his craft, build a broader understanding of digital business transformation at PS, and learn how to better collaborate with peers in other capabilities to solve many of client challenges.
